About SMS Foundation

SMS Foundation - Sreya, Manasvi, Swastik

Welcome to the SMS Foundation, a beacon of hope and compassion dedicated to the welfare of children. Formed by the visionary trio of Sreya, Manasvi, and Swastik, our foundation is deeply rooted in the belief that every child deserves a nurturing environment to thrive and fulfill their potential. Sreya's heartfelt dedication, Manasvi's genuine concern for community well-being, and Swastik's innovative solutions converge to create a formidable force for child welfare.

At SMS Foundation, we are committed to addressing the unique challenges faced by children, ensuring their rights are upheld, and providing them with opportunities for a brighter future. Our mission extends beyond mere assistance; we strive to create lasting, positive impacts that empower children to overcome obstacles and build a foundation for lifelong success. Join us in our pursuit of a world where every child's well-being is safeguarded, and their dreams are not just nurtured but realized.
